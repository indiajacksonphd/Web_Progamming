Eight Puzzle

The Eight Puzzle is a classic sliding puzzle consisting of a 3x3 grid with eight numbered tiles and an empty space. The tiles are initially randomly arranged, and the goal is to slide the tiles one at a time to rearrange them into their original order. The only legal moves are to slide a tile into the empty space, and the puzzle is considered solved when the tiles are in the order 1 to 8, with the empty space in the last position.

This game includes the follow:

1) If you complete the puzzle, you will get a congratulations animation from us!
2) When you click on a tile, you will see a slide transition!
3) When you pick a puzzle, your timer will start and your moves will be tracked!
4) Click Play Sound to hear some old school retro gamin music!
5) Choose from multiple background photos (famous gaming characters!)
6) Want more of a challenge? Try the Fifteen Puzzle here!
